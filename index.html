Miguna deported out of Kenya

Acording to a Standard news article published today morning, Miguna was surprisingly and hastily deported to Canada

under unclear circumstances. It is still unclear why and when but it comes as a shock since the high court has ordered all charges to be dropped against him until he's produced in Milimani Courts.

All kenyans can do is awe as the courts are disobedied by the executive and the NRM vs Jubilee theatrics take centre stage.
